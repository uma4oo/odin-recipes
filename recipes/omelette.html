Omelette recipe

Description:

Simple homemade dish for a good breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • a splash of water
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Preparation: Whisk the eggs with water, salt, and pepper in a bowl
  2. Cooking: In a non-stick pan, melt some butter over medium heat, pour in the egg mixture, and gently stir as it cooks
  3. Finishing Touches: Once the edges are set, you can add herbs or cheese if desired
  4. Fold the omelette in half and serve hot.
